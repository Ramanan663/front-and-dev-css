Is it just exercise?
Is it a religion, a philosophy, an ideology? Or is it something completely different? The word "Yoga" literally means "union". The word "yoga" essentially means "that which brings you to reality". Literally it means 'association'. Union that it takes you to ultimate reality, where individual manifestations of life are superficial bubbles during the creation process. At present, a coconut palm and a mango tree have emerged from the same soil. The human body and so many beings have emerged from the same earth. It's all the same Earth.
